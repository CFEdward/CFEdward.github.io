Features

This project was built according to our client Visio's requirements as a prototype in VR.


The purpose was to experiment with haptic feedback in order to provide an accessibility option for visually impaired users.


The prototype should be fully playable while not relying on sight, but on the haptic feedback that guides the player to aim towards the floating trash.
