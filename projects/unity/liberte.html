Features

An AR Museum experience built per our client Wagenborg's requirements.


It is a tool that can only be "played" at their office where the ship Liberte resides.


It features hand tracking and an interactive way to receive information about the ship during different time periods in AR.


I built systems such as the Tutorial, UI Interactions, focused on the overall immersion, as well as some custom shaders.


Additionally, I also voice acted all of the info-cards throughout the entire experience.
